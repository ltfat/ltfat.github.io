QAM4 - Quadrature amplitude modulation of order 4

Usage

xo=qam4(xi);

Description

qam4(xi) converts a vector of 0's and 1's into the complex roots of unity (QAM4 modulation). Every 2 input coefficients are mapped into 1 output coefficient.
