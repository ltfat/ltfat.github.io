DEMO_BLOCKPROC_PARAMEQUALIZER - Real-time equalizer demonstration

Usage

demo_blockproc_paramequalizer('gspi.wav')

Description

For additional help call demo_blockproc_paramequalizer without arguments.

This demonstration shows an example of a octave parametric equalizer. See chapter 5.2 in the book by Zolzer.

References:

U. Zolzer. Digital Audio Signal Processing. John Wiley and Sons Ltd, 2 edition, 2008.
