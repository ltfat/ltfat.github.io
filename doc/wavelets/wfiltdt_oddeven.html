WFILTDT_ODDEVEN - Kingsbury's symmetric odd and even filters

Usage

[h,g,a] = wfiltdt_oddeven(N);

Description

[h,g,a]=wfilt_oddeven(N) with \(N \in {1}\) returns the original odd and even symmetric filters suitable for dual-tree complex wavelet transform. The filters in individual trees are biorthogonal.

Examples:

wfiltdtinfo('ana:oddeven1');
