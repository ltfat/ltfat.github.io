Detailed Description

Utility functions

Note
All utility functions working with arrays can work inplace (i.e. in == out) without allocating additional memory internally.

Enumeration Type Documentation

enum ltfat_normalize_t
Enumerator
LTFAT_NORMALIZE_NULL 

Don't normalize

LTFAT_NORMALIZE_AREA 

Normalize to 1 norm (divide by the sum of abs. values)

LTFAT_NORMALIZE_1 

Normalize to 1 norm (divide by the sum of abs. values)

LTFAT_NORMALIZE_ENERGY 

Normalize to 2 norm (divide by the square root of sum of squares of abs. values)

LTFAT_NORMALIZE_2 

Normalize to 2 norm (divide by the square root of sum of squares of abs. values)

LTFAT_NORMALIZE_INF 

Normalize to inf norm (divide by the max abs. val.)

LTFAT_NORMALIZE_PEAK 

Normalize to inf norm (divide by the max abs. val.)
